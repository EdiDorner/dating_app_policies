We may retain deleted-account data during a recovery period of up to 90 days, during which account recovery may be available.

## 2. Data Deletion

### How to Request Account and Data Deletion

You can request deletion by either method:

1. In-app account deletion flow, if available in your app version
2. Email request to `privacy@beastaldug.com` from your registered account email

### What Happens After a Deletion Request

- Your account is deactivated and is no longer shown as an active profile in the app
- Active session, refresh, and push-notification tokens linked to the account are revoked or disabled
- Certain identifiers, such as your email address and phone number, are removed from the active account record

### Recovery Window After Deletion

Deleted-account data may be retained during a recovery period of up to 90 days after deletion to support account restoration and protect the service against abuse.

- During this 90-day window, account recovery may be available if you can verify the original account details
- After the 90-day recovery window ends, account recovery is no longer available
- After that point, we intend to permanently delete or anonymize the remaining personal data, except where longer retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, dispute, or compliance reasons

### What We May Retain Longer

- Data required for legal, tax, fraud prevention, dispute, or security obligations
- Minimal logs and authentication or recovery records required for abuse prevention and platform integrity

### Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law. If a deletion request is accepted, the 90-day recovery window described above begins when the deletion is applied.
