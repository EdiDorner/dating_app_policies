# Child Safety Standards – Beastald Dating App

## Zero Tolerance Policy

Beastald Dating has a strict zero-tolerance policy against child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

Our platform is intended exclusively for adults (18 years and older). Any content involving minors is strictly prohibited.

---

## User Safety & Reporting

Users can report any suspicious behavior, inappropriate content, or accounts directly within the app.

We encourage users to report anything that may violate our policies or put others at risk.

---

## Moderation & Enforcement

All reports are reviewed promptly.

We take immediate action against violations, including:
- Account suspension
- Permanent bans
- Removal of content

Where required, we cooperate with relevant law enforcement authorities.

---

## Prevention Measures

We actively work to prevent abuse on our platform through:
- User reporting systems
- Content monitoring
- Enforcement of strict community guidelines
